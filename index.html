Obesity trend in the US

This page demonstrates the obesity rate in America, contributing factors to the obesity rate such as fast food, and consequences of overweight

World Obesity Rate

Obesity is one of the biggest problems to many countries in the world. The United States of America has been consistently one of top countries that have highest obesity rate over population


650 mil adults are obese (WHO, 2020)

Average calories of popular fastfood categories

An average adult should consume around 2000 – 2500 calories per day. However, if the body consumes too many calories without burning excess energy, the body will store the extra calories as fat. Fast foods are the rich sources of calories and fat.

Death leading causes in the US

According to Centers for Disease Control and Prevention - CDC (2020): “People who have obesity, compared to those with a healthy weight, are at increased risk for many serious diseases and health conditions” (2020). Obese people have a higher risk of having Coronary heart disease, type 2 diabetes, stroke. According to the data from the CDC, heart diseases and diabetes have been the leading factors of death in the US consistently.
