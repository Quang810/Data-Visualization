World Obesity Rate

Obesity is one of the biggest problems to many countries in the world. The United States of America has been consistently one of top countries that have highest obesity rate over population

Fastfood restaurant locations in the US

The US has over 50.000 fast food chains (Njaramba, 2020). The fast food giant Mc'Donald alone has over 13.000 locations in the US. The Center for Disease Control and Prevention considers obesity is a major health issue resulting in numerous diseases in the US.
