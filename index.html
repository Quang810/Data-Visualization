Obesity In The USA

This page demonstrates the obesity rate in America, contributing factors to the obesity rate, and consequences of being overweight

World Obesity Rate

Obesity is one of the biggest problems to many countries in the world. The United States of America has been consistently one of top countries that have highest obesity rate over population


650 million adults are obese (WHO, 2020)

Fast-food Restaurants In The US

The US has over 50.000 fast food chains (Njaramba, 2020). The Center for Disease Control and Prevention considers obesity is a major health issue resulting in numerous diseases in the US.


13.797 MacDonald locations in the US (Scrapehero, 2017)

Calories Per Serving?

An average adult should consume around 2000 – 2500 calories per day (NHS, 2019). However, if the body consumes too many calories without burning excess energy, the body will store the extra calories as fat. Fast foods are the rich sources of calories and fat.


1.100 calories from a BigMac meal (Walsh, 2020)

Obesity Rate In The USA

According to the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (Fryar, Carroll, and Ogden, 2018), the obesity rate in the US has reached highest record in 2017 – 2018. The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention announced that all 50 states in America has over 20% obesity rate (CDC, 2020)


42.4% obesity rate(Fryar, Carroll, and Ogden, 2018)

Consequences?

Obese people have a higher risk of having Coronary heart disease, type 2 diabetes, stroke. According to the data from the CDC, heart diseases and diabetes have been the leading factors of death in the US consistently.


647.457 deaths by heart diseases in 2017 (Heron, 2019)
